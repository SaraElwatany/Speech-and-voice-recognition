Speech Recognizer

Feature Extraction

We are using the MFCC technique (Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficient) to extract the voice feature of the person.MFCC is considered the pitch of the human voice in the frequency form which scale them on the mel scale and these Features are unique to others. after extracting feature, the system is be ready for training and test the machine learning model.

Model-GMM

In the training of the model, we use the Gaussian Mixture Modeling to train the extracted features. The Gaussian Mixture Modeling is a model assumes each data cluster as a different gaussian distribution and baesd on the probability whih the data came out,it store on the data base. So in the matching process,the system will match person voice to the voice in the database.
